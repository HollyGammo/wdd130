FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

ARE RESERVATIONS REQUIRED?

Yes! Please call us at 123-456-7890 and make a reservation for the size raft you need and for how many life jackets and paddles you will need.

WHERE DO WE MEET TO START OUR RAFTING ADVENTURE?

Meet at the boat launch at the base of Flaming Gorge Damn. If you have made a reservation, we will have your raft, life jackets & paddles waiting for you.

WHAT DO WE NEED TO WEAR?

Dress for the weather. You can wear a bathing suit or jeans and a jacket, whatever you feel comfortable in. Shoes are important on the river.

IS PRIOR RAFTING EXPERIENCE REQUIRED?

No rafting experience is required. This river has several rapid areas but for the most part, the river is calm, beautiful and not hard to navigate.

WHAT IS THE CANCELATION POLICY?

You must cancel reservations within 12 hours of your departure time. No refund will be given for no shows or late cancelations.

WILL YOU DROP OFF AND PICK OUR RAFT?

Yes! We will have your raft and the other supplies waiting for you at the drop off location when you get there, and we will take care of it all when your through.

WILL WE HAVE A GUIDE WITH US IN THE RAFT?

There is no guide provided for your trip down the river. The trip will take about four hours and the end/pick-up point is clearly marked at the end.

CAN WE FISH OUT OF THE RAFT?

Yes! Bring your own fishing equipment and you can fish while you enjoy your trip down the river.

CAN WE BRING OUR DOG WITH US?

Yes! If you want your dog to join you on your trip down the river, then bring your furry best friend with you. Make sure to keep them safe on the water.

HOW OLD DO YOU HAVE TO BE?

There is no set age requirement for your trip down the river. Everyone must sign a waiver to participate, but the river is calm enough for all ages to enjoy.

DO I NEED TO KNOW HOW TO SWIM?

No. You will have a life jacket to wear so you will not need to know how to swim. The river is calm enough that not many people get thrown from the raft.

WHAT CAN I BRING WITH ME?

We recommend that you bring a water resistant pouch or bag to put cell phones, keys, etc. to keep them safe and so they don't get lost.

HOW LONG IS THE TRIP DOWN THE RIVER?

The section of the river that we run usually ends up being about a four hour trip, depending upon how high and fast the river is running.

WHAT ARE YOUR HOURS OF OPERATION?

We are open Mon.-Sat. 9 AM to 8 PM. We start our river trips in May and stop runs in mid October.
